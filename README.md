## About

I sit at an unusual intersection — **institutional finance background** (all of the certifications + Master degree in the US.) meets **solo product builder** shipping TypeScript every day. Lattice is the thesis behind that hybrid: tools should compress decision-making for people who think in spreadsheets *and* people who think in code.

7+ years across **private credit, real estate, and alternatives** at institutional shops across the world. Now spending those instincts on a different game — building infrastructure for Korea's AI builder ecosystem.

## Why Lattice

> Korea's AI builder ecosystem is growing fast — but the infrastructure to navigate it isn't. Tools are scattered across English-first sources. Prompts get lost in screenshots. MCP servers live in random Notion docs. Claude Code skills are tribal knowledge.

Lattice is a single conviction made into a product: **builders in Korea deserve a curated, native-language surface for the AI tooling stack**. No ads. No SEO bloat. Just the signal.

The "mental framework" prompt collections (PM, PE associate, SV engineer) reflect how I actually use AI day-to-day — not as a chatbot, but as a *decision compression layer*. That's what Lattice is trying to make replicable for everyone else.
